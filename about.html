About Kisii University SDA Church

Welcome to our community of faith, hope, and love. Discover our story, mission, and the vibrant spiritual family at KSUSDA.

Our Story & Community

Kisii University SDA Church (KSUSDA) was established in 2005 as a spiritual home for Seventh-day Adventist students, staff, and community members around Kisii University. What began as a small fellowship has grown into a vibrant, diverse congregation that welcomes all who seek to worship and serve God.

Located within the Kisii University campus, our church serves as both a place of worship and a center for community engagement. We are committed to nurturing spiritual growth, fostering meaningful relationships, and serving our local community with Christ's love.

As a Seventh-day Adventist congregation, we uphold Biblical teachings while embracing the unique opportunities of our university setting to engage young minds in faith exploration and service.

Our Journey Through Time

Milestones in the growth of KSUSDA

2005

Foundation of KSUSDA

The church was officially established as a fellowship group for Adventist students at Kisii University, holding services in a small campus classroom with 15 regular attendees.

2008

First Church Building

With growing membership, KSUSDA moved to its first dedicated worship space on campus, accommodating up to 100 worshippers and establishing formal church leadership.

2012

Expansion & Community Outreach

The church launched its community service programs, including health clinics, tutoring for local children, and environmental clean-up initiatives, expanding its impact beyond campus.

2016

New Sanctuary Dedication

The current sanctuary was completed and dedicated, providing seating for 300+ worshippers and modern facilities for various ministries and community activities.

2020

Digital Ministry Launch

KSUSDA embraced digital ministry with live-streamed services, online Bible studies, and virtual prayer meetings, expanding reach during the pandemic and beyond.

2023

Current Ministry Focus

Today, KSUSDA serves over 400 regular attendees with diverse ministries for students, youth, families, and community members, continuing our mission of faith, service, and fellowship.

Our Beliefs

Fundamental beliefs that guide our faith and practice as Seventh-day Adventists

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the ultimate authority for faith and practice. It reveals God's plan for salvation and provides guidance for daily living.

Salvation in Christ

We believe salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone - His life, death, and resurrection. Through grace, we are saved from sin and receive eternal life.

The Sabbath

We believe the seventh-day Sabbath (Saturday) is a holy day of rest, worship, and fellowship established by God at creation and reaffirmed in the Ten Commandments.

The Church

We believe the church is the community of believers who accept Jesus as Lord and Savior. We are called to fellowship, worship, service, and proclaiming the gospel.

Holistic Health

We believe in caring for our bodies as temples of the Holy Spirit, practicing principles of healthful living that honor God and promote wellbeing.

Christ's Return

We believe in the soon return of Jesus Christ, which will bring an end to sin and suffering, and establish God's eternal kingdom of peace and righteousness.
