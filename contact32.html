Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about KSUSDA Church

What are your worship service times?

+

Our main worship services are held every Sabbath (Saturday): Sabbath School at 8:00 AM and Divine Worship at 10AM to 1PM.From 2:30PM to 5:00 PM ,we have a bible study.5PM to 6:45PM, ALO AMO study.5:45PM to 6:00PM ,Sundown worship. We also have midweek prayer meetings on Wednesdays at 6:30 PM and various ministry meetings throughout the week.

How can I get involved in ministries?

+

We have various ministries including First Fruit, New Zion, Christ Messangers,End Time Heralds, Personal Ministry, and more. You can indicate your interest through the contact form, speak to any ministry leader after service, or visit our Ministries page for more information.

Do you have programs for students?

+

Yes! As a campus church, we have special programs for students including Master Guide, Bible study groups, mentorship programs,sign language groups, and social events. We also provide academic support and counseling services for students.

How can I submit a prayer request?

+

You can submit prayer requests through our contact form (select "Prayer Request" as the subject), email us directly, or call the church office. All prayer requests are treated confidentially and shared with our prayer team.

Also you can visit the prayer request page to submit your prayers as well

Is there parking available at the church?

+

Yes, we have designated parking areas within Kisii University for church attendees. Visitor parking is available near the main auditorium(Hall). For special events, additional parking arrangements are made and communicated in advance.
