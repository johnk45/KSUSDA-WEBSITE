Love Without Parallel

Pr. Manuel Bellina
June 17, 2025

Love Without Parallel is a message focused on the uniqueness and depth of Christ-like love. In this sermon, Pr. Bellina emphasizes that Christian love isn't ordinary affection or conditional kindness, but a selfless, divine love that reflects God's own character. It calls believers to go beyond natural responses, loving others — even the difficult and unlovable — with a love that mirrors the boundless, sacrificial love of God. This kind of love transforms relationships, overcomes barriers, and demonstrates the heart of Christ in everyday life
