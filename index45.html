About Our Church

Our Mission & Vision

KSUSDA is a spiritual home for students and staff at Kisii University, dedicated to nurturing faith through worship, fellowship, and service. Our mission is to spread the everlasting gospel and empower members to live Christ-centered lives on campus and beyond.

We believe in creating a welcoming environment where everyone can experience God's love, grow in their faith, and use their gifts to serve others.
